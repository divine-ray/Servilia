Private company specialised on high-security express delivery of any goods anywhere. 
An inofficial motto is “If it fits, it ships. Guaranteed!”.
They’re (in)famous for utilising the most bizarre strategies for delivering, such as entertaining [[Songbird|Songbirds]] and [[Praries]] for certain deliveries.
Due to being founded prior to the [[Company Clash]], they managed to hide away some of their militant assets, which see regular use to defend high value deliveries. This does include getting into combat and conflicts with other parties.
