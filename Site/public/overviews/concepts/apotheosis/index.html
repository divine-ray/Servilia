Overviews

One dedicated enough could reach beyond the mortal spaces and challenge those beyond. The road to Ascension and Godhood is the roughest imaginable; few have walked this path, and none have returned, yet those hav’ been revered as messiah of their following.

Feasting on the flesh of a marionette of such a deity would a fraction of their power, yet catapulting a mortal beyond any comprehensible space and time. To truly topple and eradicate one god, the journey is not possible. Not to a single man…

But if one were to kill god for his powers, wouldn’t that make oneself god? Wouldn’t the next one, driven mad in their frenzy, gluttony for power, devour another god-gone before?
