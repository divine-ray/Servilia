A plague, which swept the lands of Servilia. Caused by Schi ut-Pula’s depression and the devouring of Charon by the Nølkā. Affected individuals have been forcibly relocated and displaced into Sepulchre, the Undead City. Rather, a ghetto. It infects most if not all sentient beings, but mostly humanoids have been observed to succumb to it. ‘Infected’ people do not die in the traditional sense.
