Powerful objects, made as fanciful hook, line and sinker, do drag down below the fools, the unwise and the greedy. Power, wealth and other promises float and flicker on the surface, leading to the trap, one which eventually turns fatal. Promises be granted, lest they take their price in rates of interest, turning, twisting the one who fell for it. One who wants to ring the bells may do so with the striker, but only once, for it will consume them. Lest; the bells call forth woe, they are heard beyond our world.
