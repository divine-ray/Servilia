A glass-clear rock, partially encrusted with regular stone. A few impurities and cracks stain the sight, which seem to shift by themselves. It is capable of absorbing the final echo of someone’s consciousness in the case of a ultimate demise. It can also be used to chain a spirit to the material plane for easy recall, or be a beacon and navigation aid to a voluntarily contracted spirit.

Starting item to those with a bound familiar

To summon the bound spirit
chant, calling the spirit by name:

Heed these words Hear my demand I call to you, spirit from the other side You, [Spirit Name], bound to this stone I wish to speak to you here Come to me Come to this stone I call you now, (spirits name)!

To summon the imprinted echo
chant, calling the spirit by name:

Listen to my pleads Hear my mourning I call to you spirit from the realm of death I ask you kindly Come to me Wake from this stone I wake you now, (spirits name)!
