Beings

Sleek red robes, once white. Stained with the blood of those long forgotten, those fools who opposed him. He has long lost any identity, ever since his body turned itself outside, and there was machine. Steel, Brass and Bronze. The Holy Trinity of Gear; driving the innovative force behind Thylyian military and its best machinations. Granted the title of a It, it has forsaken humanity and converted to the Machine, His Divine Heart.

Its flesh has been cleansed and eradicated by its Magnum Opus, The Beast. %%Offers modifications for Thylyian Weaponry, when in disguise.%%
