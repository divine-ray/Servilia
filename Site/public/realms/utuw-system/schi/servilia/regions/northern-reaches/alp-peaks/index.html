#Areas #Northern-Reaches  These peaks seem to be haunted by poltergeists and memetic nightmares, but only during night. This may be a influence from the deviated sea. An above average occurrence of night runners have been spotted here…   Almost identical to the surface traits of the Hollow Mountains:

  • Infested with night Runners
  • Multitude of sharp mountain peaks
  • Related to a supernatural being.

However, the Night Runner presence looms much stronger here, as no other force is governing them here.

They even seem to seep through the rock and stone… It cannot be healthy to stay here for longer.. Their strong presence here haunts any unsuspecting traveller, especially in their dreams.
