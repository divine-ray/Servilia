#Children-of-the-Flame #Ruin #dungeons #Cult
Running up A Hill, there it has a lonely castle, set to rot and salvage. It’s roofing, originally copper, has been completely torn out, and some sections have been demolished. Former Elvin stronghold, destroyed in the genocidal sweep of Thylyist against the Lavish. After the devastation of it and the surrounding areas, scavengers and pillagers tore out anything they could peddle away, leaving the stone brick walls towering like husks.

Leyline node due to the souls of unrighteously deceased inhabiting it, which partially feeds the Children.

Headquarters to the Children of The Flame. Epicentre of the Scorched Acres, hard shell to the Crimson Heart, protecting it and the children from any outsiders.

Otherwise Home to a few hollowed elvs, who get supervised by benevolent gnomes/gremlins.
