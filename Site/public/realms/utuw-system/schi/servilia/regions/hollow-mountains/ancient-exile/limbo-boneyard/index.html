#Ancients #Void  

A big storage pile of Vessel Masks, carved from celestial and draconic bones, sealed away and heavily protected.

The Ancient Deities amassed Vessel Masks from failed or defeated Vessels to hinder the Void on affecting the material plane.

It’s located below Londo, dug by dwarves into the hollow mountains.

No one speaks about this, they either forgot or are too afraid of the word being spread. At some point, information got leaked and The Void caused a massive raid upon Londo with all available forces, which led to the destruction of Londo.  

 

Now threatened by Warlock Gaíz-an, who embarked on their quest to return the masks to the void.

Below Londo.
